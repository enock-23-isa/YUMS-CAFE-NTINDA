About Our Restaurant

Welcome to YUMS CAFE NTINDA, where every meal tells a story.

Our History

Established in 2023, YUMS CAFE NTINDA started as a humble family-owned restaurant with a passion for creating delicious and memorable dining experiences. Over the years, our dedication to quality and customer satisfaction has helped us grow into a beloved local hotspot in Ntinda.

Our founders, inspired by their travels and love for diverse culinary traditions, decided to bring a multi-cuisine dining experience to Ntinda. From the very beginning, our mission has been to serve fresh, high-quality meals that bring joy and comfort to our community.
